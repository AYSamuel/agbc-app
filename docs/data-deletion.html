Step 2: Identity Verification

For security purposes, we may need to verify your identity before processing the deletion request. We may ask you to:

  • Confirm additional account details
  • Respond from your registered email address
  • Verify your phone number (if provided during registration)

Step 3: Confirmation & Processing

Once we verify your identity, we will send you a confirmation email before proceeding with the deletion. After you confirm:

  • Your account will be deactivated immediately
  • All personal data will be permanently deleted from our systems within 30 days
  • You will receive a final confirmation email once the deletion is complete

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following information will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Email, password, phone number, and authentication credentials
  • Profile Data: Name, profile picture, biographical information, and church membership details
  • User-Generated Content: Tasks, meetings, prayer requests, and comments you've created
  • Activity Data: Usage logs, notification preferences, and interaction history
  • Location Data: Saved addresses and location preferences
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens and device identifiers
‚ÑπÔ∏è Note on Shared Content: Content you've shared with other church members (such as public prayer requests, task assignments, or meeting notes) may remain visible to other users for church operational purposes, but will be anonymized and no longer linked to your account.

Data Retention Exceptions

In some limited cases, we may be required to retain certain information even after account deletion:

  • Legal Compliance: Information required by law, regulation, or legal process (e.g., financial records for tax purposes)
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: Logs needed to investigate security incidents or prevent fraud
  • Backup Systems: Residual copies in backup systems (deleted within 90 days according to our backup retention policy)

Alternative: Temporarily Deactivate Your Account

If you're not ready for permanent deletion, you can request temporary account deactivation instead. Contact us at the email below, and we can:

  • Suspend your account access without deleting your data
  • Remove you from notifications and communications
  • Allow you to reactivate your account later if you change your mind

After Deletion: Creating a New Account

If you delete your account and later decide to rejoin GRACE PORTAL, you can create a new account. However:

  • Your previous account data will not be recoverable
  • You will start with a fresh account and profile
  • Church administrators will need to reassign your roles and permissions
