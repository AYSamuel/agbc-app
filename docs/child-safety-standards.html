Child Safety Standards & Protection Policy

Last Updated: December 19, 2025

Amazing Grace Bible Church (AGBC) and GRACE PORTAL are committed to providing a safe environment for all members, especially children and minors. We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and have implemented comprehensive policies and technical measures to protect children using our platform.

1. Our Commitment to Child Safety

GRACE PORTAL is designed exclusively for members of Amazing Grace Bible Church and implements the following commitments:

  • Zero Tolerance: We have zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form
  • Proactive Protection: We implement technical and administrative measures to prevent, detect, and respond to potential risks
  • Accountability: All church administrators and staff are trained on child protection policies
  • Transparency: We maintain clear reporting mechanisms and work with authorities when necessary
  • Continuous Improvement: We regularly review and update our safety measures

2. Age Requirements

Minimum Age

Users must be at least 13 years of age to create an account on GRACE PORTAL. This aligns with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) requirements.

Parental Supervision

We strongly recommend that:

  • Users under 18 use the app with parental knowledge and supervision
  • Parents/guardians monitor their children's app usage and interactions
  • Families discuss appropriate online behavior and church community engagement
  • Parents contact church administrators if they have concerns about their child's account

3. Prevention Measures Against CSAE

3.1 Technical Safeguards

  • Closed Community: The app is exclusively for verified AGBC members - not open to the general public
  • Administrator Oversight: All user registrations are reviewed and approved by church administrators
  • Role-Based Access: Content visibility is controlled based on user roles and permissions
  • No Direct Messaging: The app does not include private one-on-one messaging between members to prevent grooming opportunities
  • Monitored Content: Prayer requests, meeting notes, and other shared content can be reviewed by church leadership
  • Secure Authentication: Strong password requirements and secure login prevent unauthorized access

3.2 Administrative Safeguards

  • Background Checks: Church administrators with app access undergo appropriate background screening
  • Child Protection Training: Staff and volunteers receive training on recognizing and reporting abuse
  • Verification Process: New members are verified through existing church membership records
  • Content Moderation: Church administrators can review and remove inappropriate content
  • Account Monitoring: Suspicious account activity is flagged and investigated

3.3 Content Restrictions

The following content is strictly prohibited on GRACE PORTAL:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind
  • Sexually explicit content involving minors
  • Content that sexualizes, grooms, or exploits children
  • Inappropriate contact requests or communications with minors
  • Sharing of personal information that could endanger children
  • Any content that violates child protection laws

4. Detection and Reporting Mechanisms

4.1 How We Detect Violations

  • Administrator review of flagged content
  • User reports of concerning behavior
  • Regular audits of user interactions and content
  • Monitoring of account activity patterns

4.2 User Reporting

How to Report Concerns:

If you encounter any content or behavior that concerns you, especially regarding child safety:

  1. Immediate Safety Issues: Contact emergency services (999 UK / 911 US)
  2. App-Related Concerns: Email us immediately at oami.gospel@gmail.com with "URGENT: Child Safety Concern" in the subject line
  3. Church Leadership: Contact AGBC church leadership directly at +447342920067
  4. Anonymous Reporting: You may report concerns anonymously - we will investigate all reports

4.3 Our Response Process

When a child safety concern is reported, we will:

  1. Immediate Action (Within 1 hour): Review the report and take preliminary protective measures
  2. Investigation (Within 24 hours): Conduct a thorough investigation of the reported content or behavior
  3. Content Removal: Immediately remove any content that violates child safety policies
  4. Account Action: Suspend or permanently ban accounts involved in violations
  5. Law Enforcement Notification: Report CSAE material and serious violations to appropriate authorities
  6. Documentation: Maintain records of all reports and actions taken
  7. Follow-Up: Provide updates to the reporter (when appropriate) and implement additional safeguards if needed

5. Cooperation with Authorities

GRACE PORTAL and Amazing Grace Bible Church fully cooperate with law enforcement agencies:

  • We report all suspected CSAE material to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) CyberTipline and UK authorities
  • We preserve evidence and user data as required by law enforcement investigations
  • We comply with all legal requests for information related to child safety
  • We maintain documentation of all CSAE reports and our responses

6. User Education and Prevention

For Parents and Guardians:

  • Discuss safe online behavior with your children
  • Monitor your child's app usage and church community interactions
  • Teach children never to share personal information (address, school, phone number) in prayer requests or other content
  • Encourage children to report anything that makes them uncomfortable
  • Review your child's activity in the app regularly

For Young Users:

  • Never share personal information like your home address, school name, or phone number
  • Tell a parent or trusted adult if someone makes you feel uncomfortable
  • Do not meet anyone from the app in person without parent/guardian supervision
  • Report any inappropriate content or messages to church leadership or your parents

7. Privacy Protection for Minors

We implement enhanced privacy protections for users under 18:

  • Parental consent required for account creation for users under 13 (where applicable)
  • Limited data collection from minor users
  • Restricted visibility of minor profiles to verified church members only
  • Prohibition on sharing photos or detailed personal information of minors without parental consent
  • Parents can request deletion of their child's account and data at any time

8. Training and Awareness

AGBC ensures that all church staff and administrators with app access receive:

  • Comprehensive child protection policy training
  • Regular updates on child safety best practices
  • Clear protocols for identifying and reporting concerning behavior
  • Understanding of legal obligations regarding child safety
  • Refresher training at least annually
